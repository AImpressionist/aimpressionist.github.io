About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Mathematics and Statistics in UNSW, Australia, under the supervision of Prof. Scott Sisson.

My Ph.D degree is obtained in University of Technology, Sydney on May, 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Longbing Cao and Prof. Richard Yi Da Xu. Prior to coming to Australia, I received my Bachelor degree in Mathematical Statistics From University of Science and Technology of China in 2010.

Research

My research interests include

  • Bayesian Nonparametric Space Partitions

  • Monte Carlo methods

  • Social Networks

  • Deep Generative Models

  • Variational Inference

  • Hawkes Processes
